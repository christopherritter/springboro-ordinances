402.52 WHEELCHAIR, MOTORIZED.
=============================

"Motorized wheelchair" means any self-propelled vehicle designed for,
and used by, a handicapped person and that is incapable of a speed in
excess of eight miles per hour.

(ORC 4511.01(EEE))
