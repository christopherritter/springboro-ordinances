Section 5.01  Ordinances and Resolutions.
 

     Council action shall be by ordinance, resolution or motion. Ordinances shall be the enactments of the Council and shall have the full force and effect of law. Ordinances shall prescribe permanent rules of conduct of government. Resolutions shall be orders or declarations of the Council of a special or temporary nature.  Motions are oral proposals for Council action limited to the same purposes as resolutions.  (Amended 11-7-00.)
