Section 7.04   Administrative Code.
 

     Subject to the provisions of this Charter and after consultation with and the recommendation of the Manager, the Council may adopt an Administrative Code which shall provide in detail the organization of the City Government, and define the powers and duties of each organizational unit. Amendments to and revision of the Administrative Code shall be made by the Council only after consultation with and the recommendation of the Manager. Where the Charter and the Administrative Code are silent, the employees through the Manager of the City shall have and may exercise all powers and duties provided for similar employees by the State law.  (Amended 11-4-08.)
