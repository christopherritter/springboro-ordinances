Section 13.05  Continuation of Ordinances.
 

     All ordinances, resolutions and other acts of the Village of Springboro, in effect at the time this Charter becomes effective, shall remain in effect, except as superseded by the provisions of this Charter, until they are amended or repealed by Council or by the electors as provided in Section 5.07. In any conflict between an ordinance or resolution of Council and the provisions of the Ohio Revised Code, other than the provisions reserved by the Ohio Constitution to the Ohio General Assembly, the provisions of the ordinances and resolutions shall prevail.
