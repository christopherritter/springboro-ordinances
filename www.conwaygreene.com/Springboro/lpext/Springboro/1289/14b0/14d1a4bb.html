Section 13.08  Continuation of Contracts and Improvements.
 

     All the contracts entered into by the City or for its benefit, prior to the taking of effect of this Charter, shall remain in full force and effect. Public improvements for which legislative steps have been taken under laws existing at the time this Charter takes effect, shall be completed, as nearly as practicable, under the provisions of such laws. 

(Amended 11-4-08.)
