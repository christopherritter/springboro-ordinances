Section 9.08   Park Board.
 

     (a)     Membership.  There shall be a Park Board consisting of five (5) members who shall be electors of the City and hold no compensated position therein, appointed by the Council. Terms of office shall be two (2) years and overlapping, so arranged that at least two (2) members' terms shall expire on January 1 of each year. The Council shall fill any vacancies by appointment for the unexpired term.

     (b)     Duties.  The Board shall have all the powers and duties conferred upon it by ordinance.  (Amended 11-4-08.)
