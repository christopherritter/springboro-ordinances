Section 8.10   Amendment of Appropriations.
 

     The Council, on recommendation of the Manager, may at any time by ordinance transfer any unencumbered balance of an appropriation from one item or project to another as permitted by Ohio law, or reduce or increase any item, provided the ordinance as amended does not increase the authorized expenditures to an amount greater than the total estimated income. 

(Amended 11-7-00.)
