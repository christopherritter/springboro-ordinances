1026.04  TRIMMING AND REMOVING DEAD OR DAMAGED  VEGETATION.

     An owner of a lot or parcel of land in the Municipality shall trim or remove, as the occasion may require, every dead, decayed or broken tree, plant or shrub, or part thereof, which is at risk of falling to the street or sidewalk or of hanging so as to interfere with necessary sight distance at intersections as determined by the City Engineer.

(Ord. 98-43.  Passed 10-1-98.)
