1020.02  MAINTENANCE OF STATE HIGHWAYS.

     (a)      The consent of the Municipality is hereby given to the State Department of Transportation to maintain the traveled portions of, to centerline paint, and to plow snow and place abrasives for ice control on, State highways in the Municipality in accordance with the standard practices of the Department.

 

     (b)      The Clerk of Council is hereby directed to furnish to the Ohio Director of Transportation and the Board of County Commissioners a certified copy of this section immediately upon the taking effect thereof.

(Ord. 79A. Passed 12-29-47; Ord. 545. Passed 2-19-76.)
