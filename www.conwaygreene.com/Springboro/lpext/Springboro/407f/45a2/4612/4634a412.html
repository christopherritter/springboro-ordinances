1064.04  VSP ACCESS PROVISION.

     Upon receipt of notice from a VSP that it will begin providing video service in the City pursuant to a VSA, the City Manager or his/her designee is authorized and directed to provide such VSP with notice that the VSP shall be required to provide the same number of PEG channels in the City under the same service tier conditions and subject to the same channel reclamation conditions as may be proscribed by Ohio R.C. 1332.30(A)(1)(a-b) for the Incumbent Cable Provider with the most recent obligation in the City, which notice shall be delivered in a manner that provides for proof of timely delivery and shall state the appropriate number of PEG channels and service tiers required to be provided by the VSP within the City within 120 days after delivery of such notice.  Additionally, should no PEG channels currently be provided by an Incumbent Cable Provider with such an obligation in the City, the City Manager may provide written notice to a VSP of its obligation to provide PEG  channels in accordance with Ohio R.C. 1332.30(B)(1).

(Ord. 07-43.  Passed 11-15-07.)
