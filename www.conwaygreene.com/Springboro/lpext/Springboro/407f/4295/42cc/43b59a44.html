1042.31  YANKEE ROAD UTILITY SERVICE AREA, ADDITIONAL SEWER AND WATER TAP-IN FEES.

     (a)      The City Manager is hereby authorized and directed on behalf of Council to establish within the benefitted area a service district to be known as the Yankee Road Utility Service Area which shall contain the Brookside subdivision and the surrounding area designated on Exhibit "A" of original Ordinance 92-45. The Manager is directed to define and establish exact boundaries for the service area consistent with the area outlined on such Exhibit "A".
