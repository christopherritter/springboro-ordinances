832.04  WAIVER OF LICENSE FEE.

     The Manager or other chief administrative officer may, at his or her discretion, grant, without cost, a license for the holding of a circus, carnival, sideshow or musical or minstrel entertainment  for not more than six consecutive days, where all of the performances are fostered  and  supervised  by civic interests of the Municipality and where  a substantial part of the funds derived therefrom is expended or is to be expended for charitable or civic purposes.
