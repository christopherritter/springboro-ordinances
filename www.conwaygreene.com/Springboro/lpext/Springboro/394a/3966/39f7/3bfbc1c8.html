826.99  PENALTY.

     Whoever violates any provision of this chapter shall be guilty of a misdemeanor of the third degree and shall be fined not  more than five hundred dollars ($500.00), or imprisoned not more than sixty days, or both. Each day that a violation continues shall be deemed a separate offense and shall be punishable as such hereunder.

(Ord. 96-38. Passed 7-11-96.)
