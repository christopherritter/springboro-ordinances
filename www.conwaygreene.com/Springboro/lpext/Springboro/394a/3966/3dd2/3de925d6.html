850.01   PURPOSE.

     It is the intent of the Council in enacting this chapter to improve the quality of life and economic vitality of the City of Springboro; enforce income tax regulations, prevent safety and fire hazards; disturbances, disruption of traffic, disorderly assemblies, and gambling within the City; to establish standards for licensing, regulation and control of Computerized Internet Sweepstakes Terminal Cafés and premises upon which they are located and operated.

(Ord. 11-9.  Passed 4-7-11.)
