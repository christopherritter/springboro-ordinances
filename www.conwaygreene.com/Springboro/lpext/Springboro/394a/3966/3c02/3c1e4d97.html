828.02  UNINVITED PEDDLING AND SOLICITING ON RESIDENTIAL PROPERTY.

     No solicitor, canvasser or peddler shall go in or upon any residential property in the Municipality without first being invited to do so by the owner or occupant of such property, for the purpose of selling or offering for sale, or offering to take orders for, goods or chattels of any kind.

(Ord. 400A. Passed 9-30-70.)
