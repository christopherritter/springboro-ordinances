1262.11  CONFORMITY WITH BUILDING PERMITS AND ZONING  CERTIFICATES REQUIRED.

     Building permits or zoning certificates issued on the basis of plans and applications approved by the Building and Zoning Inspector authorize only the use, arrangement and construction set forth in such approved plans and applications, and no other use, arrangement or construction. A use, arrangement or construction at variance with that authorized shall be deemed a violation of this Zoning Code, punishable as provided in Section 1262.99.

(Ord. 546B. Passed 2-25-76.)
