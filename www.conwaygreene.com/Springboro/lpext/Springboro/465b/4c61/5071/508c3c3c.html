1268.03 IDENTIFICATION OF SCHEDULE.

     The Schedule of District Regulations shall be identified by the signature of the Mayor, attested by the Clerk of Council, and bear the seal of the Municipality under the following words: "This is to certify that this is the Schedule of District Regulations referred to in Section 1268.02 of the Codified Ordinances of the Municipality of Springboro, Ohio," together with the date of the adoption or amendment of this Zoning Code.

(Ord. 546B. Passed 2-25-76.)
