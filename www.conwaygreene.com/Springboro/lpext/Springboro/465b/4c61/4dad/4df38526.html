1264.055 R-2A  LOW DENSITY RESIDENTIAL REVISED DISTRICT.

     The purpose of the R-2A District is to provide land for single-family housing units not to exceed four per acre, with smaller dwelling unit sizes than are permitted in the R-2 District. Commercial and industrial development is prohibited. Group or central water and sewer facilities are required.

(Ord. 1032. Passed 5-5-82.)
