1266.02  AUTHENTICATION OF MAP.

     The Official Zoning Map shall be identified by the signature of the Mayor, attested by the Clerk of Council, and bear the seal of the Municipality under the following words: "This is to certify that this is the Official Zoning Map referred to in Section 1266.01 of the Codified Ordinances of the Municipality of Springboro, Ohio," together with the date of the original adoption of this Zoning Code.  

(Ord.  01-13.  Passed 3-15-01.)
