1244.05  AMENDMENTS TO PRELIMINARY PLAN.

     At any time after preliminary plan approval and before submission of a final plan, the applicant may request of the Director of Planning that an amendment is made in the approval or conditional approval of the preliminary plan. Under regulations established by the Planning Commission, the Director of Planning may agree to proposed amendments that are deemed to be minor. If the proposed amendment is major, the Planning Commission shall follow the same requirements for preliminary plan approval found in Section 1244.04. The Planning Commission shall approve or disapprove any proposed major amendment and may make any modifications in the terms and conditions of preliminary plan approval reasonably related to the proposed amendment. If the applicant is unwilling to accept the proposed major amendment under the terms and conditions required by the Planning Commission, the applicant may withdraw the proposed major amendment. A major amendment is defined as any change in density, open space, street or lot layout. A fee as defined in the City of Springboro Schedule of Permit Fees outlined in Section 1464.04 of the codified ordinances of the City of Springboro shall accompany the proposed subdivision amendment.

(Ord. 04-41.  Passed 8-19-04.)
