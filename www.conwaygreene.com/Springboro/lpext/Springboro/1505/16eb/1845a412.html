CHAPTER 244
Employees Generally
 

EDITOR’S NOTE: Former Codified Ordinances 244.00-244.43 set forth the Employee Policies and Procedures Manual and established fringe benefits for City employees.  Ordinance 10-39 passed December 2, 2010, adopted an amended Employee Policy Manual as a separate Manual that is not contained in the Codified Ordinances.  Copies of the Employee Policy Manual adopted by Ordinance 10-39, and any permitted amendment thereto, are on file in the office of the Clerk of Council.
