290.03  MAYOR'S COURT IMPROVEMENT FUND.

     (a)      There is hereby created a fund to be known as the Mayor's Court  Improvement Fund. All applicable costs received as Mayor's Court  Improvement Fund costs, shall be paid to the City's Director of Finance and maintained as a separate fund.
 

     (b)      Expenditures shall be made from the Mayor's Court  Improvement  Fund to finance the computerization and/or the acquisition and/or maintenance of legal research service for the Mayor's Court, including, but not limited to, computer space; computer electrical; computer air-conditioning; computer furniture; computer printer, computer software, subscription to computer service(s) staff to operate the computer system, including fringes; supplies, i.e. computer paper etc; training expenses; maintenance of equipment; and computer needs studies.

(Ord. 94-12. Passed 5-5-94.)
