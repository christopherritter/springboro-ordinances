642.28  FOOD STAMPS.

     (a)     No organization, as defined in Section 606.08(d), shall:

          (1)     Knowingly allow an employee to sell, transfer or trade items or services, the purchase of which is prohibited by the “Food Stamp Act of 1977", 91 Stat. 958, 7 U.S.C.A. 2011, as amended, in exchange for food stamp coupons; or

          (2)     Negligently allow an employee to sell, transfer or exchange food stamp coupons for anything of value.
 

     (b)     Whoever violates this section is guilty of illegal use of food stamps, a misdemeanor of the first degree, provided the face value of the food stamp coupons involved in the violation is less than two hundred dollars ($200.00) and provided the offender has not previously been convicted of a violation of the “Food Stamp Act”.  Punishment shall be as provided in Section 698.02.  (ORC 2913.46)
