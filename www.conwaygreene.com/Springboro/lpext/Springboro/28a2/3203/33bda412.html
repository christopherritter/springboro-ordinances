642.32 ETHNIC INTIMIDATION.     

     (a)     No person shall violate Ohio R.C. 2903.21, 2903.22, 2909.06, 2909.07 or 2917.21(A)(3) to (5) or Sections 636.04, 636.05, 636.15(a)(3) to (5), 642.10 or 642.11 of the General Offenses Code by reason of the race, color, religion or national origin of another person or group of persons.
 

     (b)     Whoever violates this section is guilty of ethnic intimidation.  Ethnic intimidation is an offense of the next higher degree than the offense the commission of which is a necessary element of ethnic intimidation. 

(ORC 2917.12)

     Punishment shall be as provided in Section 698.02.
