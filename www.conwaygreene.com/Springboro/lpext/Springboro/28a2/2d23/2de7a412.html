624.09  CONTROLLED SUBSTANCE OR PRESCRIPTION LABELS.

     (a)     No person shall alter, deface or remove any label affixed by a manufacturer, wholesaler, pharmacist or licensed health professional authorized to prescribe drugs who dispenses a controlled substance in a package or container, as long as any of the original contents remain, except when lawfully filling a prescription.  (ORC 3719.08)
 

     (b)     Whoever violates this section is guilty of a misdemeanor of the first degree.  If the offender has previously been convicted of a violation of this section, Ohio R.C. 3719.07 or 3719.08 or a drug abuse offense, such violation is a felony and shall be prosecuted under appropriate State law.  (ORC 3719.99)
