660.15  DRAINING SLOPS.

     (a)     No person shall drain, cause to be drained or allow to drain from any property occupied by him or her, any kitchen slops or other greasy or impure matter into the open gutters or waterways in the Municipality, unless such drainage has been drained into a vault and filtered through a lesser vault filled with sand and fine gravel, built under the inspection of the County Board of Health.  (1977 Code Sec. 94.20)
 

     (b)     Whoever violates this section is guilty of a minor misdemeanor.  Punishment shall be as provided in Section 698.02.
