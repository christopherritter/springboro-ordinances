1464.03  INSPECTION OF PUBLIC IMPROVEMENTS; BILLING.

     The Director of Finance shall bill all developers for necessary inspections incident to the construction of streets, sidewalks, storm sewers, utilities and grading,  as set forth in Appendix A of Chapter 1464 of the Codified Ordinances for each hour or part thereof in which inspection services are provided.  (Ord. 03-42.  Passed 8-21-03.)
