1262.12 ISSUANCE OF ZONING CERTIFICATES FOR PROJECTS REQUIRING SITE PLAN REVIEW.

The Building and Zoning Inspector shall not issue a zoning certificate for any application requiring site plan review by the Planning Commission and/or the Board of Zoning Appeals, namely planned unit developments and mobile home parks, unless the site plan has been approved by the Commission and/or the Board. (Ord. 546B. Passed 2-25-76.)
