646.09 HOURS.

(a) The park properties shall be open during the following hours, or as otherwise designated and posted by order of the City Manager:

April 1 through October 31 6:00 a.m. to 10:00 p.m.

November 1 through March 31 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.

(b) No person shall enter onto or remain on park property other than during the hours listed in subsection (a) hereof without a City of Springboro activity permit.

(Ord. 95-28. Passed 6-15-95.)
