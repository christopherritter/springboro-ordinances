1424.99 PENALTY.

Whoever violates or fails to comply with any of the provisions of the Insulation Requirements, as adopted in Section 1424.01, is guilty of a misdemeanor of the fourth degree and shall be fined not more than two hundred fifty dollars ($250.00) or imprisoned not more than thirty days, or both, for each offense. A separate offense shall be deemed committed each day during or on which a violation or noncompliance occurs or continues.

(Ord. 90-18. Passed 4-5-90.)
