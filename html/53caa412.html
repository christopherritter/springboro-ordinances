1272.15 PRE-APPLICATION CONFERENCE RECOMMENDED.

To obtain information regarding the PUD approval process, requirements, and other information relative to the PUD application, each applicant is highly encouraged to engage in informal consultations with the Planning Commission or its designated officer prior to filing an application. The applicant should be prepared to discuss the general concept of the PUD, with a conceptual plan. Thereafter, the Planning Commission or its designated officer shall furnish the applicant with written comments regarding such conference, including appropriate recommendations to inform and assist the applicant prior to his or her preparation of the components of the PUD zoning application. It is not required that a person requesting a preapplication conference be a legal owner or holder of an equitable interest in the subject property.

(Ord. 02-4. Passed 1-17-02.)
