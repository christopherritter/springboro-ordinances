636.10 SUSPENSION OF SENTENCE ON POSTING BOND.

Sentence may be suspended if a person, after conviction under Section 636.09 and before sentence thereunder, appears before the court in which such conviction took place and enters into bond to the Municipality in a sum fixed by the court at not less than five hundred dollars ($500.00) nor more than one thousand dollars ($1,000) with sureties approved by such court, conditioned that such person will furnish such child or other dependent with necessary or proper home, care, food and clothing, or will pay promptly each week for such purpose to a trustee named by such court, a sum to be fixed by it.

(ORC 3113.04)
