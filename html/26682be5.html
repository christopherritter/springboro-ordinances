446.01 DECALS AND IDENTIFYING NUMBERS REQUIRED.

No person shall operate, nor shall any person, being the owner thereof or having supervisory responsibility therefor, permit the operation of a school bus in the Municipality unless there is displayed thereon the decal issued by the State Highway Patrol under Ohio R.C. 4511.761 and the identifying number required by Ohio R.C. 4511.764.

(ORC 4511.761; ORC 4511.764; 1977 Code Sec. 73.25)
