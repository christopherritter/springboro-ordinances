668.14 PERIODIC INSPECTIONS.

Independent of the annual clean-up and inspection provided for in Sections 668.09 through 668.13, the County Health Officer or the Chief of Police shall make periodic inspections of properties in the Municipality and report all violations of any of the provisions of this chapter to the Manager who shall order such nuisances to be abated as provided in Section 668.13. The penalty provided in Section 668.09 and the remedy provided in Section 668.11 for noncompliance with the resolutions of Council shall also apply to noncompliance with the orders of the Manager under this section.
