1240.11 PUBLIC PURPOSE.

Regulation of the subdivision of land and the attachment of reasonable conditions to land subdivision is an exercise of valid police power delegated by the State of Ohio to the City of Springboro. The developer has the duty of compliance with reasonable conditions laid down by the Planning Commission and City Council for design, dedication, improvement and restrictive use of the land to conform to the physical and economic development of the municipality and to the health, safety and general welfare of the future lot owners in the subdivision and of the community at large.

(Ord. 04-41. Passed 8-19-04.)
