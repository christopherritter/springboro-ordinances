1020.01 CONDITIONS PRECEDENT TO IMPROVING STREETS.

No department of the Municipality shall accept, lay out, open, improve, grade, pave, curb or light any street or other way, unless such street or way has been accepted or opened or otherwise received the legal status of a public street or way or unless such street or way corresponds in location and extent with a street or way shown on a recorded plat which has been legally accepted by Council. (1977 Code Sec. 90.01)
