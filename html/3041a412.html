634.04 CONDUCT OF OWNERS OR OPERATORS OF ESTABLISHMENTS.

(a) No owner or operator of an establishment or his or her agent or employee shall knowingly permit a minor to remain upon the premises of such establishment during the curfew hours. (Ord. 402A. Passed 10-15-70.)

(b) Whoever violates this section is guilty of a minor misdemeanor. A separate offense shall be deemed committed each day during or on which a violation occurs or continues. Punishment shall be as provided in Section 698.02.
