1026.05 NOTICE TO CORRECT.

If any owner is deemed to be in violation of Section 1026.01, 1026.02, 1026.03 or 1026.04 hereof, he or she shall be given ten days written notice to cure such violation, such notice to be served upon the owner in conformity with Ohio R.C. 731.52. Such notice shall include notification of the right to a hearing before the City Manager within the ten day conformance period. (Ord. 98-43. Passed 10-1-98.)
