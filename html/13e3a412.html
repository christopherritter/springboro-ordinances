Section 7.03 Department Directors.

At the head of each department there shall be a director who shall be appointed by the Manager with the approval of Council and can be removed by the Manager with the approval of Council. Two or more departments may be headed by the same person and the Manager may serve as the director of one or more departments in addition to his or her duties as Manager, if the Council approves. The Director shall have supervision and control of the department he or she heads subject to the direction of the Manager. (Amended 11-4-08.)
