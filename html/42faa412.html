1042.01 DEFINITIONS.

As used in this chapter:

(a) "Service fee" and "tap-in fee" mean the fee charged for having a water system available for use by persons wishing to build in the Municipality. Such fee is determined by the cost of wells, pumping, facilities, storage, water mains, etc.

(b) "Tap" and "tap-in" mean physical connection made into the Municipality owned water system. (Ord. 942. Passed 3-19-81.)
