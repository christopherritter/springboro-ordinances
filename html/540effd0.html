1272.18 REVISION OF APPROVED GENERAL PLAN.

The final development plan shall substantially conform to the approved general plan. In the preparation of the final development plan, the applicant shall not materially deviate from the approved general plan or to the related documents. Major changes to the general plan (as defined in Section 1272.02) may not be made unless and until a revised general plan is processed and approved in accordance with this chapter. Minor changes may be approved by the Planning Commission without submission of the revised general plan to City Council.

(Ord. 02-4. Passed 1-17-02.)
