824.01 LICENSE FEES.

Each proprietor of a billiard or pool table or of a bowling alley, or a combination of both, shall pay an annual license fee as set forth in Appendix A of Chapter 1464 of the Building and Housing Code of the Codified Ordinances for each such table, as set forth in such Appendix A of Chapter 1464 for the first such alley and as set forth in such Appendix A of Chapter 1464 for each additional alley.

(Ord. 93-46. Passed 7-1-93.)
