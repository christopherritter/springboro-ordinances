1244.05 AMENDMENTS TO PRELIMINARY PLAN.

At any time after preliminary plan approval and before submission of a final plan, the applicant may request of the Director of Planning that an amendment is made in the approval or conditional approval of the preliminary plan. Under regulations established by the Planning Commission, the Director of Planning may agree to proposed amendments that are deemed to be minor. If the proposed amendment is major, the Planning Commission shall follow the same requirements for preliminary plan approval found in Section 1464.04 of the codified ordinances of the City of Springboro shall accompany the proposed subdivision amendment.

(Ord. 04-41. Passed 8-19-04.)
