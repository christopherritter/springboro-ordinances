1478.01 DRAINAGE FROM EXISTING SWIMMING POOLS.

All existing swimming pools that are not connected to a drainage facility approved by the Board of Health or permissible under the Unified Building Code shall be modified or operated as to dispose of water from those pools in the following manner:

(a) Such water shall be piped to the nearest available storm sewer; or

(b) Such water shall be disposed of on the zoning lot upon which it is situated and in such a manner that it does not drain onto public streets, sidewalks or other public or privately owned land.

(Ord. 90-41. Passed 7-5-90.)
