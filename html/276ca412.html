452.21 PLACEMENT OF SNOW ON CITY STREETS AND ALLEYS PROHIBITED.

(a) No person shall dump, shovel, blow or otherwise transfer snow from private property onto the public streets or alleys of the City.

(b) Whoever violates this section is guilty of a minor misdemeanor, and shall be assessed any costs incurred by the municipality in removing and disposing of snow illegally placed on the public streets or alleys.

(Ord. 95-64. Passed 11-16-95.)
